TDS–CALCIUM PROPIONATE (CHEMSINO)

Distilled Monoglyceride
CAS#31566-31-1
DESCRIPTION
Distilled monoglyceride is a white powder lipophilic non-ionic surfactant with 90% monoglycerides content. It has effects of emulsification, dispersion, foaming, defoaming, starch anti-aging and fat agglomeration control. It is derived from Kosher certified materials for wider acceptance of use.
APPLICATION
Distilled monoglyceride is widely used in foodstuffs as thickening and emulsifying agent for margarine, shortenings, and otherfood products; flavorings; emulsifying agent for oils, waxes and solvents. It can also be used as protective coating for hygroscopic powders; cosmetics; opacifier and as resin lubricant.
USAGE
Recommended to be used at level of 2%. Formulators can vary amount depending on its end application.
RECOMMENDED STORAGE
This product will remain stable for 18 months from date of production provided that it is stored at ambient conditions away from moisture inlet and direct sunlight. For stored products beyond 1 year, consult chemsino industry . for recommendation of use.
MATERIAL SAFETY HANDLING INFORMATION
Distilled monoglyceride although is not harmful, contact with skin and eyes should be avoided and necessary personal protective equipment and clothing should be worn, such as chemical gloves, gas mask, eye goggles and apron. Keep away from heat and any source of ignition. “Chemsino has Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) for each products.
