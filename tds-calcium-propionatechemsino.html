TDS–CALCIUM PROPIONATE (CHEMSINO)

Calcium Propionate
CAS#4075-81-4
DESCRIPTION
As a food Preservatives, it is listed as E number 282 in the Codex Alimentarius. Calcium Propionate is used as a preservative in a wide variety of products, including but not limited to bread, other baked goods, processed meat, whey, and dairy products. In agriculture, it is used to prevent milk fever in cows and as a feed supplement. Propionates prevent microbes from producing the energy they need.
APPLICATION
Calcium propionate is widely used in foodstuffs as preservative and mold inhibitor.
USAGE
Preservative; mildew preventive
Usable range and use: Limited dough wet products: 0.25g/kg. Cheese: 0.3g/kg. Bread, western pastry, vinegar, bean product, cake, soy sauce: 1g/kg.
Packaging: 25kg kraft bags; special packing can be arranged.
RECOMMENDED STORAGE
In cool, dry and well-ventilated place, moisture-proof. Avoid direct sunlight and toxic/harmful substances.
MATERIAL SAFETY HANDLING INFORMATION
Calcium Propionate is not harmful, but contact with skin and eyes should be avoided. Use protective gloves, goggles, apron, and avoid heat sources or ignition. MSDS available upon request.
