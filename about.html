Who Is Sgutrade and
What Do We Do?

We purchase, transport and transform crops, connecting
farmers to consumers, touching millions of lives daily.

Nature’s Power, Unlocked

ADM partners with farmers in the world's most productive agricultural regions to support their businesses, open and maintain markets, and ensure their crops are meeting the needs of customers spanning food, feed, fuel and consumer and industrial products.

Real-World Outcomes

From meals for families to restoring soil health to nutrition education, ADM Cares drives change, and measures results to prove impact.

Food Security

Every shared meal brings us closer to better access to food and creating stronger communities.

Sustainability Practices

By improving soil health, restoring hectares, and offering training and support, we’re helping create more resilient land for future generations

Farmer Resilience

By expanding practices that drive positive farm economics, we help strengthen the resiliency of the agriculture and food sectors.

Health and Well-Being

We’re empowering the next generation through access to wholesome meals and healthy habits.
