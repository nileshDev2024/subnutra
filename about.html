Who Is SGU Trade and
What Do We Do?

SGU Trade sources, transports, and delivers high-quality
food ingredients, connecting producers, manufacturers, and consumers
while ensuring safe, reliable, and innovative supply solutions every day.

WHO WE ARE

SGU Trade Pvt Ltd. has an extensive portfolio of food ingredients that includes bakery, dairy, prepared foods, confectionery, pet foods, beverages, health, and nutritional products.

SubNutra is driven by an industry expert team with expertise in distribution and quality. Our team is able to provide you with a huge range of ingredients that will meet all your requirements. SubNutra is a proud supplier of quality ingredients to some of the biggest food manufacturer companies of India & globally. Our aim is to deliver you the best quality & reliable products.

What We Do

Manufacturer

We are a leading manufacturer of quality food ingredients in India, producing specialty ingredients for dairy, bakery & other industries.

Importer

In order to deliver the finest products, SubNutra imports food and beverage ingredients across the globe.

Exporter

We export a wide range of superior quality food ingredients & products worldwide.

Distribution

SubNutra is a trusted wholesale distributor committed to providing competitively sourced, exotic food & beverage ingredients.

Trader

We sell raw materials and food ingredients to our customers active in confectionery, dairy, beverages and other food industries.

Supplier

SubNutra Food Ingredients Pvt. Ltd. is a professional ingredient supplier for the Food and Beverage industries.

Research & Product Development

We check, test, develop & research ingredients & products to provide the finest quality food ingredients to our clients.

SGU Trade is committed to providing top-quality ingredients and solutions that improve lives and empower industries globally.
